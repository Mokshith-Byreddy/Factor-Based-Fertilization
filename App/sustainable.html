Sustainable Fertilization Practices

Learn how to maintain soil fertility while preserving the environment.

Use cover crops and conservation tillage to enhance soil health.

Harvest Season: July - August

Harvest Duration: 2 months

Soil Type: Loamy to sandy soil

Fertilizers: Nitrogen-rich fertilizers

Pest Management: Integrated Pest Management (IPM) practices

Nutrient Management: Regular soil testing and application of organic matter. Use cover crops to enhance soil fertility. Incorporate crop residues to improve soil organic matter. Apply micronutrients as needed to prevent deficiencies.

Implement alternate wetting and drying (AWD) irrigation to conserve water.

Harvest Season: September - October

Harvest Duration: 1-2 months

Soil Type: Clayey soil

Fertilizers: Balanced NPK fertilizers

Pest Management: Biological control and resistant varieties

Nutrient Management: Use of green manures and organic fertilizers. Implement crop rotation with legumes to enhance nitrogen fixation. Monitor nutrient levels regularly to adjust fertilization strategies.

Rotate with legumes and use organic amendments for better soil fertility.
