Soil Testing Methods

To determine your soil's nutrient content, consider the following methods:

  • DIY Soil Test Kits: Available at garden centers, these kits allow you to test pH, nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels.
  • Laboratory Testing: For comprehensive analysis, send a soil sample to a local agricultural extension office or a laboratory.
  • Home Remedies: Use vinegar for acid testing (bubbles indicate acidity) and baking soda for alkalinity (bubbles indicate alkalinity).
